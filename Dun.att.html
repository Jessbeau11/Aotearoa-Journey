Dunedin - attractions

Dunedin has a lot of attractions and activities you can do during your time here from walking around the gorgous gardens to the steepest street in the world (baldwin street). This photo is from clip & climb which is a trampoline place but has recently added a wall climbing area, this is a great place to visit and see how high you can go!

Dunedin - attractions

The museum is a great place to go to learn about the history of Dunedin but also NZ as a whole. My favourite place in the museum has to be the animals attic which holds many animals in a taxidermy state. There are crabs, sharks, lions, butterflies and peguins. The other place i reccomend is activity world, this place is science related, it has lighting and optical illustions and even a big slide.
