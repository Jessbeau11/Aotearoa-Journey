Queenstown - Transportation

If you want to take a trip to Queenstown, I highly reccomend taking a car so you can explore the landscape that overlooks the small town. If you get the chance to drive from Kingston through the gourge you will be able to see the bluest water in NZ with the highest hills and prettiest nature.

Queenstown - Transportation

Whenever my family does a road-trip up to Queenstown one of my favourite parts about the trip is the drive up. The scenery is always stunning and the weather can be anywhere from freezing snow to a hot day.
