WELCOME to my blog

Hi, I'm Jess! I've been exploring the incredible landscapes of New Zealand for over five years, and I'm passionate about sharing the hidden gems and must-see destinations that make Aotearoa so special.

From the snow-capped peaks of the Southern Alps to the pristine beaches of the Bay of Islands, join me as I uncover the stories, cultures, and adventures that await in this remarkable country.

Whether you're planning your first visit or you're a seasoned traveler, you'll find insider tips, stunning photography, and detailed guides to help you make the most of your New Zealand journey.
