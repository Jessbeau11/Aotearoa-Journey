Queenstown - attractions

if you want to take a trip on one of the last steam powered ships then now is your chance. In Queenstown they have a boat called the EARNSLAW. This boat was built in 1911 and has been going ever since. However the people of Queenstown have decided that they needed to remove one of the key features of the boat (coal-power) because it was bad for the environment. The boat is still up and running and I try to go on the boat every time im in the town.

Once on the boat it will take you on a on scenic 90-minute lake cruises across Lake Wakatipu to Walter Peak High Country Farm and back. You can chose to take the boat to the island and have high tea in an historial builds and feed the farm animals or take the boat back to the main land.
