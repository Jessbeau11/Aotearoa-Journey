Wellington - attractions

Wellington has many attractions and activities to do during your time there. I would reccomend going to the Wellington zoo, this zoo was so fun when i went in 2021. I was lucky enough to be able to feed the red pandas, they have big claws and tuff fur. They are also much heavier than i thought they would be.
